Ice cream Flavour

Ice cream flavors are sweet tastes added to a frozen dairy or plant-based base.
They range from simple classics like vanilla and chocolate to fruit, nut, and wild savory options.
There’s nothing like a cold and creamy treat to beat the heat, but we could indulge in ice cream any time of year.
We have dairy farmers to thank for this favorite dessert — you can’t have ice cream without dairy! For a dessert to officially be considered ice cream,it must contain at least 10 percent milkfat.
