Big spoon creamery

At Big Spoon, we’re really passionate about two big ideas: making the best possible ice cream,
and using it as a vehicle to positively impact our community. We are proud to offer handmade artisanal ice cream with a focus on local
and seasonal ingredients. We’re committed to making the highest quality ice cream possible with the best ingredients and
an impeccable attention to detail. Our journey started in 2010 after meeting while working
under James Beard Award Winning Chef Frank Stitt. We eventually started dating and got married in April 2014.
Three months later, Big Spoon was born as a pop-up in our driveway. This quickly evolved into an ice cream cart, then a truck,
and eventually our first brick-and-mortar location in Avondale in 2017. Two years later, we opened our second location in Homewood,
and then a third in Huntsville in 2023. When you visit our shops, we hope you have the best ice cream of your life. But more than that, our hope is that you experience meaningful hospitality and make lifelong memories with the people that you love.
